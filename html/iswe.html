Interest Subsidy for Women Entrepreneurs

The Interest Subsidy for Women Entrepreneurs scheme was launched by the Government of Karnataka in 2015-16 to provide financial assistance to women entrepreneurs in the small and medium industries and services sector.

Scheme Overview

  • Loan Amount: ₹5 lakh to ₹200 lakh (₹5 lakh to ₹2 crore).
  • Interest Rate: 14% per annum.
  • Interest Subsidy: The Karnataka State Women’s Development Corporation (KSWDC) pays 10% of the interest for five years.
  • Leisure Period: 12 months before the repayment of the principal amount starts.
  • Eligibility: Women entrepreneurs planning to start small or medium enterprises in Karnataka.

Benefits of the Scheme

  • Encourages more women to enter entrepreneurship by reducing financial burdens.
  • Provides easy access to capital for women-led businesses.
  • Ensures long-term financial sustainability by subsidizing interest payments.
  • Boosts employment opportunities for women in small and medium enterprises.

Application Process

  1. Visit the nearest Karnataka State Financial Corporation (KSFC) branch.
  2. Submit a detailed business plan and required documents.
  3. The application will be reviewed based on viability, profitability, and eligibility criteria.
  4. Upon approval, the loan amount is disbursed, and the interest subsidy applies.
  5. Repayment of the principal amount starts after the 12-month leisure period.

Required Documents

  • Business Plan
  • Identity Proof (Aadhar Card, PAN Card, Passport)
  • Address Proof (Electricity Bill, Ration Card, Voter ID)
  • Business Registration Certificate
  • Bank Statements (last 6 months)
  • Income Tax Returns (last 2 years, if applicable)

Other Women Entrepreneurship Schemes in Karnataka

1. Udyogini Scheme

Started in 1997-98 and revised in 2004-05, this scheme supports women in setting up micro-enterprises.

  • Loan Amount: ₹1,00,000 to ₹3,00,000
  • Subsidy: 30% for general category women, 50% for SC/ST women.
  • Eligibility: Women aged 18-55 years with an annual family income below ₹1.5 lakh.

For details, visit: Udyogini Scheme

2. Vriddhi Scheme

A government initiative for startups run by women entrepreneurs.

  • Loan Amount: ₹5 lakh to ₹10 lakh at 4% interest.
  • Repayment: 60 monthly installments.
  • Eligibility: Women-led startups in Karnataka with a turnover below ₹100 crore.

For details, visit: Vriddhi Scheme

3. Swavalambane Karnataka Programme

Supports rural women entrepreneurs in non-farm businesses.

  • Benefits: Financial aid between ₹3 lakh to ₹15 lakh.
  • Eligibility: Women-led businesses in rural Karnataka.

For details, visit: Swavalambane Karnataka Programme

Conclusion

The Interest Subsidy for Women Entrepreneurs scheme plays a crucial role in promoting women-led businesses in Karnataka. By reducing the interest burden, the scheme encourages women to take up entrepreneurial ventures and contribute to economic growth.

For more information, visit the official Karnataka State Financial Corporation (KSFC) website.
