Unnati Scheme

Unnati is a unique initiative by the Department of Social Welfare, Government of Karnataka, designed to provide a comprehensive entrepreneurship platform for startups. The scheme aims to support startups from idea identification to validation, seed funding, and market access.

Objectives

  • To provide financial assistance to SC/ST entrepreneurs.
  • To encourage innovative tech-based solutions.
  • To support startups in the fields of health, education, sanitation, waste segregation, and mechanized scavenging.
  • To create opportunities for marginalized communities in Karnataka.

Financial Support

Under the Unnati Scheme, the Karnataka government provides up to ₹50 lakh financial support to winning entrepreneurs for developing tech-based solutions.

The total investment under the scheme is ₹20 crore.

Categories of Beneficiaries

  • 1st Category: SC/ST entrepreneurs with pioneering ideas, irrespective of the domain.
  • 2nd Category: Startups from any community, provided their innovation addresses real-world problems in health, education, sanitation, waste segregation, or mechanized scavenging.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Startups must have an innovative tech-based idea/solution.
  • Must be registered as an entity (Pvt Ltd/OPC, LLP/Partnership, Proprietorship) in Karnataka.
  • At least one co-founder must belong to the SC/ST community and hold a substantial share.
  • The SC/ST co-founder must remain in the business for at least one year after receiving benefits.
  • The co-founder’s ownership should not be altered to their disadvantage.
  • The startup’s total revenue should be less than ₹50 crore since incorporation.
  • At least 50% of the workforce must be employed in Karnataka.
  • Technology-based startups must be less than four years old, while biotechnology-based startups must be less than seven years old.
  • Startups applying under social impact must justify how their innovations help the SC community.

Exclusions

  • Government employees are not eligible.
  • Beneficiaries of this scheme cannot avail benefits from other similar schemes.

Application Process

  1. Visit the official website: karnataka.gov.in.
  2. Click on the "Login" option.
  3. Enter a valid email ID and mobile number, then click "Send OTP."
  4. Enter the OTP received on the registered mobile number to log in.
  5. Follow the instructions and fill in personal details.
  6. Choose the startup funding option for SC/ST applicants.
  7. Complete the application form, upload the required documents, and submit.

Documents Required

  • SC/ST Certificate of the entrepreneur.
  • Proof that at least 50% of the workforce is qualified.
  • Startup registration documents.
  • Aadhar Card number.
  • PAN number of the founders.
  • Startup presentation deck.
  • Incorporation or registration certificate.
