Stree Shakti Scheme

The Stree Shakti Yojana, launched by the Government of India in October 2000, is a flagship scheme aimed at empowering women entrepreneurs across the country. This initiative provides financial aid and support to women who aspire to start or expand their businesses, promoting economic independence, job creation, and socio-economic development.

Features of the Stree Shakti Yojana Loan

  • Financial assistance up to ₹50 lakhs for women entrepreneurs.
  • No collateral required, making loans more accessible for women without substantial assets.
  • Interest rate concession of 0.50% for loans exceeding ₹2 lakhs.
  • Flexible repayment terms of up to 36 months, including a one-month moratorium.
  • Applicants must have at least 50% ownership of the business.

Benefits of the Stree Shakti Yojana Loan

  • Empowers women entrepreneurs by enabling economic independence.
  • Promotes job creation and boosts local economies.
  • Encourages financial inclusion by providing loans without collateral.
  • Supports skill development and capacity-building through entrepreneurship training programs.

Impact of the Stree Shakti Yojana

Since its inception, the scheme has provided financial assistance to thousands of women, enabling them to establish successful businesses. The initiative has created jobs, fostered innovation, and contributed to economic growth, helping break gender barriers in entrepreneurship.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Applicants must be women entrepreneurs.
  • Must own at least 50% of the business.
  • Participation in Entrepreneurship Development Programs (EDP) organized by state agencies is required.
  • Open to startups and existing enterprises across various sectors.

Application Process

  1. Gather necessary documents, including identity proof, address proof, income proof, and a detailed business plan.
  2. Download the online application form from the State Bank of India website.
  3. Print and fill out the application form.
  4. Submit the completed application form along with required documents to the nearest SBI branch.
  5. Applications will be evaluated based on business viability and applicant eligibility.
  6. Upon approval, loan funds will be disbursed to eligible women entrepreneurs.

Required Documents

Identity Proof

  • Voter ID card
  • Passport
  • Driving license
  • PAN card

Address Proof

  • Telephone bill
  • Property tax receipt
  • Electricity bill
  • Voter’s ID card
  • Company registration certificate
  • Company partnership registration certificate (for partnership firms)

Income Proof

  • Balance sheets (last 3 years)
  • Income statements (last 3 years)
  • GST returns (last 3 years)

Business Plan

  • Projected financials for at least 2 years (for working capital loans)
  • Profile of the business enterprise
  • Names of promoters, directors, and partners
  • Business type, facilities, and premises details
  • Shareholding ratios
  • Lease agreements (if applicable)
  • Ownership title deeds

Conclusion

The Stree Shakti Yojana is a transformative initiative that empowers women entrepreneurs by providing financial assistance, skill development, and business support. By encouraging women to break barriers and start their own businesses, the scheme plays a crucial role in driving economic growth and social development in India.
